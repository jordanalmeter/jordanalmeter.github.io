Research

My research interests began in Coxeter groups and cluster algebras, working with my advisor Dr. Nathan Reading. This led to a thesis on P-graph associahedra and hypercube-graph associahedra; the image to the right is an example of a hypercube-graph associahedron, an analog to the graph associahedron defined by repeatedly truncating faces of a hypercube.
