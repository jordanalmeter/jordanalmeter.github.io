Research

I do my research in combinatorics, and am generally interested in Coxeter groups and cluster algebras. Recently, I have been doing research in generalizing graph associahedra and nestohedra; while these polytopes are generated by iterated truncation of a simplex, any simple polytope can be truncated in a similar matter.
